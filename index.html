What you get

A force multiplier for SOC and compliance teams.

SentraCore is designed to reduce busywork, accelerate investigations, and improve security outcomes through automation and intelligence.

AI-Powered Threat Prioritization

SentraCore unifies signals from cloud, endpoint, and on-prem systems, then uses AI to prioritize threats so teams focus on what matters most.

Centralized Log Onboarding

Ingest logs from agreed sources and normalize them into a standard event schema for search, dashboards, detections, and investigations.

Live Alerts and Investigations

Run severity-based alerting across authentication anomalies, brute force attempts, suspicious access patterns, configuration risks, and other security signals.

Playbook-Style Guidance

Give analysts drill-down views into alerts, recommended remediation steps, and optional ticketing workflows such as Jira.

HIPAA-Focused Reporting

Track compliance across Access Controls, Audit Controls, Integrity, and Transmission Security with downloadable PDF reporting.

Continuous Detection Tuning

Reduce false positives over time with customer-specific tuning, alert reviews, and ongoing improvements based on operational feedback.
